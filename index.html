Short Bio:

I'm currently a Ph.D candidate at College of Information Engineering, Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, China. I'm now under the supervision of Prof. Ronghua Liang. During my Ph.D study, I worked as a research intern at Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology under the supervisor of Prof. Huamin Qu from February 2013 to July 2013. After that, I went to Internet Graphics Group, Microsoft Research Asia to continue my research on visualization with Yingcai Wu. Now I am visiting The Institute for Visualization and Interactive Systems (VIS) headed by Thomas Ertl at the University of Stuttgart. I received my Bachelor's degree in Compute Science and Technology with second major in Automation from Zhejiang University of Technology in 2010.

Research Interest:

At present, I'm interested in exploring large data using interactive visual analytics.
